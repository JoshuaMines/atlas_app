THE ATLAS APP!

An app that helps your figure out how close you are to joining the 1000lb club




A short history and explanation

"A man's worth is dictated not by the size of his estate nor the goods therin, but by his entry into the 1000 pound club."
-Thomas Jefferson

History

The 1000 pound club is a ficticious club for weight lifters whose combined squat, bench and deadlift is over 1000 pounds

How it works

Enter your 1RM squat, bench and deadlift into the field below. Click the "PRESS ME" button and it will tell you how close you are
to your target goals (see "lifting goals"). Check the bottom of the page for some additional lifting resources
